Introduction

The Career Services & Industrial Linkages Office at Barani Institute of Information Technology (BIIT), Rawalpindi, is dedicated to helping students and graduates succeed in their career and bridging the curriculum of CS with IT industry. As the oldest and best computer science institution in the area, BIIT has always been a prime target for potential employers. Historically, BIIT collaborated with ROSE IT Park in Rawalpindi to assist fresh graduates. In 2022, Prof. Dr. Jamil Sawar, the Director of BIIT, established the Career Services & Industrial Linkages Office to approach the software industry for internship program of our students. Now, Careers Services Department & Industrial Linkage engages students in comprehensive career planning and counselling activities. These include arranging lectures, workshops, and seminars on career counselling, as well as organizing on-campus recruitment drives with introductory sessions, tests, and interviews.

Objectives

The main objective of the Career Services & Industrial Linkages Office is to facilitate and support students and enhance the curriculum of Computer Science for the requirement of IT industry. This office also facilitates alumni in the career planning that will lead them to a successful future. Here are our main objectives:

    To organize meetings with industrial partners to create internship programs that will facilitate BIIT's students and contribute to improve curriculum to meet the requirements of IT industry.

    To help BIIT's graduates and current students find internships and attend career-building workshops across the country.

    To set up on-campus recruitment events where BIIT's graduates and current students can meet potential employers.

    To provide career advice to BIIT's students, including resume writing workshops, interview preparation, and career development sessions.

    To assist BIIT's students in applying for national and international scholarships for higher studies.

Industrial Partners

At BIIT, we proudly collaborate with leading industrial partners to bridge the gap between academia and industry. Our partnerships foster a dynamic exchange of knowledge, resources, and opportunities, empowering students with real-world experience and innovation. In addition, our Student Job Fair provides a platform for students to connect directly with industry leaders, explore career opportunities, and showcase their talents. Together, we drive excellence, create impactful career pathways, and contribute to the growth and success of the tech industry.
